What this is

Two honest recipes for the same product — an affordable, granite-safe, grease-cutting all-purpose cleaner — built so you can make both, spray them on the same greasy stove, and pick a winner with your own hands instead of on paper.

The only real question

Both are granite-safe and both use one refined ingredient. They differ on one axis:

Option ①

Solvent-Forward

water · coconut-sugar cleanser · orange oil. Three ingredients. The gentlest, cleanest-label option — grease comes from the surfactant plus a concentrated citrus-solvent dose. No baking soda (that's what keeps it streak-free and film-free).

Ingredients to buy

Optional grease upgrade: add a small bottle of lauryl glucoside and use it in place of ⅓ of the decyl — it cuts grease a touch harder (same "coconut-sugar cleanser" label). Not required for the test.

Two formulations to mix

Make both so you can feel the ceiling. Each fills one 16 oz spray bottle. Use warm distilled water.

①-A · Base medium

  • Warm distilled waterto 16 oz
  • Decyl glucoside3 tsp
  • Sweet orange essential oil35 drops

①-B · Boosted stronger

  • Warm distilled waterto 16 oz
  • Decyl glucoside4 tsp
  • Sweet orange essential oil45 drops

Make it — checklist

Heads up: mixed, unpreserved, this keeps ~2–4 weeks. Don't mix with any other cleaner. Keep out of reach of kids & pets.
Option ③

Coconut Sulfate

water · sodium coco sulfate · baking soda · orange oil. The stronger, cheaper cleaner. Same granite-safe near-neutral pH — the trade is the word "sulfate" on the label. This test is where you find out if that trade buys you enough extra grease power to be worth it.

Ingredients to buy

Two formulations to mix

Each fills one 16 oz bottle. SCS is potent and high-foaming — start on the low side.

③-A · Base medium

  • Warm distilled waterto 16 oz
  • Sodium coco sulfate1.5 tsp
  • Baking soda1 tsp
  • Sweet orange essential oil35 drops

③-B · Boosted stronger

  • Warm distilled waterto 16 oz
  • Sodium coco sulfate2.5 tsp
  • Baking soda1 tsp
  • Sweet orange essential oil45 drops

Make it — checklist

Heads up: SCS powder can irritate eyes/lungs — handle dry powder carefully. Mixed, it keeps ~2–4 weeks. Never mix with bleach or other cleaners.
